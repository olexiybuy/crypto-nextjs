How to Make Money with Polymarket
On Polymarket, you can make money by correctly predicting the outcome of real-world events. Here's how:

Buy Contracts Cheaply: When you believe an event is more likely to happen than the current market price suggests, buy the corresponding contract at that lower price.

Hold Until Resolution: Wait for the event to occur. Once the outcome is verified on the blockchain, the market settles.

Receive Payouts: If your prediction was correct, the contract pays out $1 per token you hold. For example, if you bought a contract for $0.50 and the event happens, you earn $1, making a profit of $0.50.

Sell for Profit: Alternatively, you can buy contracts at a low price and sell them later at a higher price if market sentiment shifts, profiting from the price difference.

Example:Suppose you think a certain team will win a match, and the contract is trading at $0.40. If they do win, the contract pays out $1, and you make a profit of $0.60 per token. You can also sell your contract before the event if its price increases, locking in gains.
